Relaxation

Spa & Wellness

Step into a world of tranquillity at our luxury spa. Our expert therapists offer a menu of traditional Ayurvedic treatments, aromatic oil massages, body wraps, and facials designed to rejuvenate your body, mind, and soul.

Facilities include private treatment rooms, a steam room, a sauna, and an outdoor meditation pavilion surrounded by tropical gardens.

Infinity Swimming Pool

Our breathtaking infinity pool stretches towards the horizon, creating a seamless blend of water and sky. Swim laps, relax on poolside loungers, or enjoy handcrafted cocktails from our swim-up bar.

A separate children's pool ensures fun for the little ones while parents unwind in the adults-only section.
